The large harbour simulator of MarineSafety Rotterdam.

The large harbour simulator of MarineSafety Rotterdam consists of 360° visuals, 3D sound and a moving platform (the pilothouse) with two degrees of freedom. The computer system consists of 22 SGI Crimsons and a DEC Vax. MarineSafety International is a wholly-owned subsidiary of FlightSafety International, providing simulator training for ships deck officers and engineers, Marine pilots, and Naval officers. Other MSI centres are located in Kings Point, NY; Newport, RI; San Diego, CA & Valejo,CA.

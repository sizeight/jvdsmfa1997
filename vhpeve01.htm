The central objective of YIELD (computer animation & installation) was to develop the iconography of the overall project and to test the design credibility of the digital models.

DATA FIELD was conceptualized to be experienced in one of the harbour simulator facilities of MarineSafety International. Instead of a ship's bridge, the platform will become a fictional, harvester-cabin.

THE VIRTUAL HARVESTER project will be presented several times in public forums during the second half of 1997.
